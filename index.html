Magic Cake

Magic cake is one of the tastiest the world has ever known.Few have tried it and lived to tell about it, and these are their stories.

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 pound of sugar
  • 3 sticks of butter

Directions

  1. Mix eggs, sugar and butter in a large bowl
  2. Spread into a non-stick dish
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour
  4. let sit at room temperature for 20 minutes
  5. Eat and enjoy!
